2025 is the inflection point. Driven by regulatory clarity (e.g., MICA, GENIUS Act), enterprises are moving from pilots to production for corporate treasury stablecoin adoption, tokenized deposits, and RWA tokenization. The goal is tangible operational benefits: cutting settlement times from days to seconds (e.g., commercial paper now settles in 93 seconds), unlocking 24/7 liquidity, and achieving significant cost reduction by removing correspondent intermediaries.

Solutions

Our modular platform delivers secure, scalable, and enterprise-grade blockchain solutions tailored to the evolving needs of the financial industry.

Institutional Custody Solutions

Implement enterprise custody solutions with multi-signature wallets, certificate-based administration, and policy-as-code governance (IBM ODM). Exportable data supports robust auditing and proof-of-reserves reporting. (Operational benefit: Institutional-grade controls and security without sacrificing operational speed.)

Payments & Treasury Automation

Leverage stablecoin payment rails for efficient cross-border payments, real-time settlements, and 24/7/365 operations. Automate liquidity management and working capital optimization; achieve significant cost reduction vs. correspondent banking (industry: up to 80% fee reduction reported in select corridors).

Tokenization-as-a-Service

Launch asset tokenization platforms for RWAs, stablecoins, and tokenized deposits. Enable T+0 Delivery-versus-Payment (DvP) capabilities, fractional ownership, and access to global, always-on markets, capitalizing on the projected $4 trillion RWA opportunity.

Blockchain Interoperability

Bridge TradFi and digital-first finance with native ISO 20022 & FIX adapters. Support payment orchestration, embedded finance, and digital wallets across public and permissioned chains, ensuring seamless integration with legacy systems.

Smart Contract Management

A generic, unified gateway to deploy, parse, register, and invoke contracts on any supported network. Monitor events for DeFi/issuance solutions and enforce complex conditions using programmable money.

Developer & API Integration

Accelerate your financial technology transformation with an API-first architecture (gRPC/REST), Swagger UI, and SDK patterns. Facilitates Open Banking, BaaS, and core banking modernization. Integrate KYC/AML automation and RegTech hooks into existing stacks.
