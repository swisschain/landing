The core data entities represent foundational components crucial for managing blockchain networks, accounts, and transactions.

These elements enable seamless integration, operation, and oversight of blockchain-related activities.

  • Blockchains: Represent different blockchain protocols (e.g., Bitcoin, Ethereum, Ripple) and network types (private, test, public).
  • Custodies: Configurations for tenants, encompassing attributes like name, type, API keys, approvers, and operational settings.
  • Broker Accounts: Multi-currency, multi-blockchain accounts aggregating personalized accounts, managing deposits, withdrawals, and balances for efficient fund management.
  • Accounts: Linked to broker accounts, these multi-currency accounts support the same blockchains and enable integration with external systems using reference IDs.
  • Deposits & Withdrawals: Record incoming and outgoing fund transfers with detailed attributes such as assets, amounts, fees, and current states.
  • Smart Contracts: Registered blockchain contracts with metadata enabling method execution via APIs, enhancing user interaction and operational flexibility.
  • AML (Anti-Money Laundering): Compliance mechanisms ensuring transfers meet regulatory standards, with results stored for transparency.
  • Payments: Store data related to invoices and merchants, streamlining the payment process within the tenant's broker accounts.

Solutions

Our modular platform delivers secure, scalable, and enterprise-grade blockchain solutions tailored to the evolving needs of the financial industry.

Institutional Custody Solutions

We provide comprehensive, multi-layered custody infrastructure with flexible wallet structures, including Hot, Warm, and Cold storage options. Our governance frameworks and customizable approval workflows are designed to meet the specific needs of institutional clients.

Payments & Treasury Automation

Our stablecoin-based global payments infrastructure ensures fast and secure transactions across borders. Reconciliation processes are fully automated and seamlessly integrated with ERP systems such as SAP and Oracle, all while complying with ISO 20022 messaging standards.

Tokenization-as-a-Service

Enterprises can quickly digitize real-world assets using our streamlined tokenization platform. We support the full token lifecycle from issuance to management and offer secure off-chain settlement using the CAST framework.

Blockchain Interoperability

Our solutions effortlessly connect traditional finance with blockchain ecosystems. With broad support for multiple blockchain networks and a universal API, integration is seamless and efficient.

Smart Contract Management

We simplify the deployment and management of smart contracts through automated event monitoring and triggered executions. Our solution also supports cross-chain functionality, enabling smart contracts to operate across various blockchain platforms.
